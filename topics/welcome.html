Welcome

My name is Farshad Ghassemi Toosi; I have a PhD in Computer Science and I am a lecturer in the Department of Computer Science at Munster Technological University (MTU) in Cork Ireland. I have a bachelor of science in Software Engineering and a Master of Science in Information Systems. I graduated with a PhD degree from Computer Science and Information Systems (CSIS) department in University of Limerick in 2017. I was a Post-doctoral researcher at Lero (The Irish Software Research Centre) in University of Limerick for 2 and half years (2017 - 2019). My main research interests include:
  • Software Engineering: Feature Location, Source Code Analysis, Clean Code Programming.
  • Data Visualization: Graph/Network visualization, multidimensional data visualization.
  • Artificial Intelligence: Optimization Algorithms (Genetic Algorithms), NLP, Data (Text/Graph) Embedding, Machine Learning.
